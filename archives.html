Scripts/Notebooks/Simulations:
  • A MATLAB routine to verify the formula for Landau-Zener transition probability
  • Common magnetic traps for ultracold atom experiments and the Majorana spin-flip problem. Link to pdf. Here are three MATLAB files with simple simulations of the common quadrupole trap, TOP trap, and all-coil Ioffe-Pritchard trap which are used to produce the figures in the pdf. (under construction)
  • PID control theory and example. This MATLAB routine simulates PID tuning for a driven, damped harmonic oscillator. This Mathematica notebook contains some Bode plots showing the effects of tuning the PID parameters. Here is a quick guide to PID feedback control.
  • Zeeman hyperfine splitting. A simple MATLAB routine for computing hyperfine splitting due to an external magnetic field. The calculation takes into account both $A_\text{hfs}$ and $B_\text{hfs}$ hyperfine-structure constants.
  • Convolution powers. A simple MATLAB routine for computing iterative convolutions of a complex function that is finitely-supported on $\mathbb{Z}^d$.
  • Power-law decay of convolution powers. This MATLAB routine shows the power-decay of convolution powers as predicted by theory.
  • Attractor of convolution powers. This MATLAB routine computes the attractor of convolution powers by numerically integrating highly oscillatory integrals.
  • Turing patterns. A MATLAB routine for generating 2D Turing patterns from the Brusselator and the Gray-Scott model.
  • Spherical Solution + Vainshtein radius. Spherical solution to non-linear massive gravity and the origins of the Vainshtein radius.
  • Cartesian Solution. Derivation of the $tt$ equation in general relativity.
  • General relativity with xACT. Some examples on how to use xACT for general relativity.
  • Einstein Field Equations from variational principles.
  • Ramsey fringes in Mathematica (interactive)
